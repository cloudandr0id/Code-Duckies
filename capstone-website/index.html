Welcome!

Our team is developing a visual programming tool for the Duckietown platform. This allows children with no prior programming experience to learn to program self-driving cars! It will be used to teach kids at a summer camp course taught by our sponsor, Dr. Truong Nghiem, and hopefully lead to greater interest in the field of autonomous vehicles!
